Big 5 circuit

Drive through the famous Rift Valley which is home to the most famous touristic destinations.Holds most of the beautiful lakes, mountains ,plains and wildlife

ITINERARY DAY 1 - NAIROBI TO AMBOSELI

  • Drive Southeast via Mombasa road which is Kenyas busiest road.Loads of economic, farming activities along the road.
  • Arrive at hotel for lunch and spend evening for leisure.
  • Set off for evening safari. Great photographic opportunity.

DAY 2 - AMBOSELI MORNING AND EVENING DRIVE

  • After breakfast ,set off for the early morning safari and return for early lunch.
  • After leisure, set off for evening safari.
  • Drive back to lodge after sundowner for dinner and rest.

DAY 3 - AMBOSELI TO NAIVASHA

  • Early breakfast, sett off for early morning safari.
  • Drive to Naivasha.Stop over at the Mai Mahiu for scenic views of The Gregorian Rift Valley. Great farming activities, wildlife and scenic views along the road.
  • Arrive hotel for lunch and refreshments.
  • Set off for boat safari and crescent island visit.Great birding photographic experience.
  • Drive back to Hotel for Dinner and rest.

DAY 4 - NAIVASHA TO NAKURU

  • Drive up to Nakuru and arrive at the hotel/lodge for lunch and refreshment.
  • Set off for evening safari.Great opportunity to view White Rhinos and loads of wildlife concentrated near the lake.
  • Drive to hotel for late dinner and early bed.

DAY 5 - NAKURU TO MAASAI MARA

  • After breakfast, drive through the Mau route to the famous Mara.Great farming activities and scenic views of the Mau escarpments.
  • Arrive at the hotel for lunch and set off for evening safari.Great opportunity to see cats and loads of wildlife.
  • Head back to the hotel for dinner and rest.

DAY 6 - FULL DAY IN MARA

  • After breakfast set off for full day drive with packed lunch.Visit the famous Mara river, short optional hikes along the river as you view different crossing points used by Wildebeests.
  • Do a bush picnic lunch and enjoy the therapy of the Savannah as you enjoy your delicacy.
  • Drive back to the hotel for dinner and rest.

DAY 7 - MARA TO NAIROBI

  • After breakfast, drive to Nairobi with packed lunch.
  • Stop over for refreshments
  • Arrive Nairobi, transfer to airport.
  • Fly home.
