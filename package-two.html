Bush To Beach Safari/Honey Moon Safari

  • Highlights
  • Itinerary
  • Includes/Excludes

Highlights

Enjoy the ultimate wilderness experience combined with the breathtaking views of powder white sand beaches. Diani beach has been ranked best in Afrika by CNN. Perfect for honeymooners.

Itinerary

DAY 1 - AIRPORT TRANSFER

  • Upon arrival, pick up by one of our guides and transferred to desired hotel. Refresh and enjoy day at hotel. Overnight at hotel.

DAY 2 - NAIROBI TO OLPEJETA

  • After breakfast, drive via Nanyuki to Olpejeta. Scenic views of wheat plantations and ranches along the way.
  • Arrive hotel at around 1pm for lunch and evening leisure. Set off for afternoon safari at around 3:30 pm. Olpejeta is East Africaâ€™s leading conservation reserve.Home to more than 1000 mammals, it boasts massive population of about 80 endangered Black rhino, Special 5 (Gravy Zebra,Reticulated Giraffe, Generuk, Somali Ostrich, Beiser Oryx). Have a chance to see Chimpanzees in East Africa,roaming free in their conservation areas.
  • Return from game drive at sunset ready for dinner and overnight stay.

DAY 3 - OLPEJETA FULL DAY SAFARI

  • After breakfast, set off for full day safari with packed lunch. Spend time searching for animals enjoying the breathtaking landscapes.
  • Picnic lunch will be served at around lunchtime. Continue safari then head back to hotel.
  • Evening leisure and early dinner. Overnight stay at hotel.

DAY 4 - OLPEJETA TO NAKURU

  • After breakfast, drive to Nakuru and drive to hotel/ lodge for lunch.
  • Set off for evening safari at around 4pm.Great birding opportunity, beautiful landscapes driving by the shores of the lake Nakuru.One of the best places to view the endangered White Rhino.
  • Return to hotel just at sunset for early dinner and overnight stay.

DAY 5 - NAKURU TO MAASAI MARA

  • Drive to the mara after breakfast via Mau escarpment.Great views of wheat plantations and flat savannah along the way.
  • Arrive hotel for lunch and evening leisure.
  • Set off for evening safari and return to lodge at sunset.Maasai mara is famous for abundant wildlife and good chance to view cats. Great Maasai culture experience. Breathtaking savannah.
  • Return to the lodge at sunset for early dinner and overnight stay.

DAY 6 - MAASAI MARA FULL DAY

  • Set off for a full day safari with packed lunch from lodge.Drive to the famous Mara River crossing point as you spot animals.Picnic lunch will be served at around 1pm.
  • Return to lodge at 3pm for afternoon leisure and overnight stay.

DAY 7 - MAASAI MARA TO NAIROBI

  • After breakfast, drive to Nairobi.
  • Optional Maasai village visit at additional cost.Great cultural experience.
  • Arrive Nairobi and head to the airport for flight transfer to Mombasa.
  • Transfer from airport in Mombasa to designated hotel.

DAY 8 AND 9 - BEACH HOLIDAY

  • Two full days on beach holiday at the resort booked on Half board with own leisure activities.Breakfast and dinner included.

DAY 10 MOMBASA TO NAIROBI.

  • After breakfast, transfer to airport to catch flight to Nairobi.
  • Arrive Nairobi and transfer to JKIA
  • Fly back home.
