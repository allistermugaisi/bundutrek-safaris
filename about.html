About Bundutrek Safaris

How We Started

Introduction

Bundutrek Safaris was curated by Mr. Bill Kibet Towett who was born and raised in Kenya. Nature has always been special to him as he was exposed to a boundless love and passion for wildlife through profound experiences while in the kenyan wilderness from a young age. This invoked a deep therapeutic feeling of wellness and harmony with nature which words could not express. He found it necessary to share his experiences on social platforms which unexpectedly, attracted a ward of attention from various audiences. He consequently, received numerous requests to join him on safari. After seeking professional advice from like-minded individuals, he gained enough knowledge and courage to spearhead numerous safaris that naturally gave rise to Bundutrek Safaris. A tour company birthed from Passion with an aim to share magical and meaningful experiences to everyone.Join me in sharing the beauty of mother nature. Nature heals.

Who We Are/What We Do

We are a private safari company, based in Nairobi,Kenya. We offer privately guided and handcrafted safaris in East Africa all managed by a workforce that is passionate and enthusiastic about nature, travel, guest relations and photography. By combining our intimate knowledge of touristic destinations, we are able to offer our guests, a wide variety of services that are detail-oriented, based on their personal preferences. Throughout the safari,our ground-teams work in-sync so as to offer safaris that are safe, comfortable and memorable.

Our Philosophy

At our safari company, we believe that responsible tourism can have a positive impact on both people and the planet.

Ecofriendly

Ecofriendly tourism promotes sustainable practices, minimizes environmental impact, supports local communities, preserves natural resources, and fosters cultural appreciation for a responsible and ethical travel experience.

Customer satisfaction

Customer satisfaction in tourism is achieved through personalized experiences, excellent service, fulfilling expectations, attentive staff, seamless operations, memorable moments, and a genuine commitment to meeting and exceeding travelers' needs and desires.

Equity

Equity in tourism emphasizes fair distribution of benefits, inclusivity, and equal opportunities for all stakeholders, including local communities, marginalized groups, and employees, ensuring everyone can participate and benefit from the tourism industry's growth.

Steadfast communication

Steadfast communication in tourism involves clear and timely information exchange, active listening, responsiveness, transparency, and effective coordination among all stakeholders to ensure smooth operations, address concerns, and provide a positive and seamless travel experience.
